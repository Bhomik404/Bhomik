My Favorite Hobby : Painting

A hobby is something you truly enjoy in your free time.I like doing painting and drawing in my free time. Colouring with paintbrushes and using my creativity provide me immense joy. I love painting with my siblings and portraying my happy family.

A set of caryons, markers or acrylics always fills me with joy. I always participate in Painting competitions in my school and have won many of them. It always motivates me to work harder. I think having a hobby makes everyone's life happier.
